Matar Paneer Recipe

Description

Matar Paneer is a popular Indian dish of cottage cheese aka Paneer and green peas cooked in a spicy and flavorsome curry

This matar-paneer is a filling, delicious and hearty vegetarian dinner with tons of North Indian flavours. This is a rich, spicy, creamy and tasty curry that your entire family will enjoy if served over steamed basmati rice, any flavored rice, Butter naan, chapati or flatbread of your choice

Ingredients

  • Cottage Cheese
  • Green Peas
  • Garam Masala
  • Whole Spices
  • Kasuri Methi
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger and Garlic
  • Cashews
  • Cream

Steps

  1. Heat a pan and add i tbsp oil. When the oil turns hot, add
    • 1 to 2 green caramoms (optional)
    • 1 inch ginger (chopped)
    • 3 garlic cloves (chopped)
    • Saute just for a minute
  2. Add 1 cup cubed onions. saute them until they turn pink to light golden.
  3. Then add 1½ cup chopped tomatoes, ½teaspoon salt and 12 whole cashew nuts.
  4. Saute for 2 to 3 mins. Cover the pan. Cook on a medium flame until the onions and tomatoes turn mushy. Turn off the stove.
  5. Cool this mixture then add ¼ to ⅓ cup curd (yogurt) for a rich and tangy flavour (optional) and grind it to a smooth puree without adding water(make sure it doesn't have grits)
  6. Now heat a pan with 2 tbsps oil.
    • Add 1 inch cinnamon
    • Add a small bay leaf
    • Half teaspoon cumin seeds
    • Fry them for a minute
  7. Reduce the heat to low.
    • Add ¼teaspoon turmeric,
    • ¾ to 1 tbsp red chilli powder,
    • ¾ to 1 tbsp garam masala
    • ½ to ¾ tbsp coriander powder
  8. Mix well (do it on a low heat so that spices doesn't get burnt)
  9. Pour smooth onion tomato puree (if not then pass it through a strainer to make it smooth)
  10. Saute all of these until the mixture comes together & turns thick. Keep stirring often.
  11. Add ¾ cup boiled green peas and pour 1 to 1¼ cups water to make a gravy.
  12. Mix well and cook until the gravy thickes then add crushed 1 tbsp kasuri methi.
  13. Add 250 grams cottage cheese and cook just for 1 to 2 mins.
  14. Turn off the heat. Add chopped coriander leaves and 3 to 4 tbsp cream. Mix well
  15. Now its ready to be served. Enjoy.
